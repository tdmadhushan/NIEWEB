Courses Offered

  • Bachelor of Education (BEd)

    Duration: 2 years

    Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in any field with minimum 50% marks

    Subjects: Pedagogy, Psychology, Teaching Strategies, Curriculum Development

  • Master of Education (MEd)

    Duration: 2 years

    Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in Education or related field with minimum 55% marks

    Subjects: Research Methodology, Educational Leadership, Assessment and Evaluation, Special Education

  • Diploma in Early Childhood Education (DECE)

    Duration: 1 year

    Eligibility: 10+2 or equivalent

    Subjects: Child Development, Play-based Learning, Curriculum Design, Classroom Management
